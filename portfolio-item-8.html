Course Project BMI Calculator

UIKit Project


I created a Body Mass Index calculator app that allowed users to calculate their body mass based on their weight and height, providing them with a piece of health advice depending on their results.

Throughout the development process, I gained knowledge on creating multi-screen apps with animated navigation and utilizing concepts such as optional binding, optional chaining, and the nil coalescing operator.

The project also required me to gain a deeper understanding of formatting strings and color literals, which allowed me to enhance the app's visual design and ensure it was both functional and visually appealing.
