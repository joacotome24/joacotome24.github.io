Course Project Xylophone

AVFoundation & UIKit Project


In this project, I had the opportunity to dive into a simple iOS recipe - how to play sound and use the AVFoundation library. I gained valuable knowledge on how to solve programming problems by exploring StackOverflow and Apple Documentation and learning how to search for solutions effectively.

Throughout the development process, I gained a deeper understanding of how to incorporate custom features into an app and make it do precisely what I wanted it to do. This project allowed me to showcase my programming skills and demonstrate my ability to create high-quality, visually appealing mobile applications.

The main objective of the project was to create a colourful Xylophone app that would allow users to play music on their mobile devices. Music apps are incredibly popular on the App Store, and this project provided me with an excellent opportunity to develop my own musical instrument application.
