Personal Project Practical Listing

CoreData & CocoaPods Project


Practical Listing is an iOS app that helps people with organization. The app provides a section where users can create different list categories. When users open a category, they can add items to a beautiful tableView. The app allows users to check off items they have already obtained or completed, delete them, or delete the entire list. Additionally, there is a search bar that enables users to search for specific items on a list, making it even easier to find what they are looking for.

To ensure the app is easy to use, CoreData and SQLite are used to persist data. The app is designed to be simple yet useful, and it has all the necessary functionality to keep users organized. The app's intuitive and straightforward interface allows users to quickly get organized and stay on top of their tasks.

Overall, Practical Listing is a successful iOS app that helps users stay organized in their daily lives. It provides a simple and effective solution for managing tasks and to-do lists.
